The Problem

Banking apps are passive

  • They show data, they don't think
  • Fraud detection is reactive — you find out after the damage
  • Spending, loans, investments, insurance, tax — all siloed
  • Most people don't know their debt-to-income ratio or financial health
  • You can't talk to your bank at 2am and get a real answer

What if your banking app
wasn't an app —

but an agent?

and acts on your behalf
